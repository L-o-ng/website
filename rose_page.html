Computing in Medicine

Technology is being used more and more within the field of medicine and is having incredible effects already. As computing in medicine advances, it promises to revolutionize the medical field, empowering healthcare professionals and enhancing patient outcomes.

Applications of technology in medicine:

  • Medical imaging and diagnostics
  • Electronic health records
  • Telemedicine
  • Wearable health devices
  • Robotic surgery
